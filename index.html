Welcome to Jaitpal Singh Anita Devi Inter College

The institution was established in the year 2019 with a vision of giving children of Hazratpur (a vilage about 40 kilometers away from Budaun) a dream education comprising of improving skills, enhancing hidden talents, building confidence, introducing academic excellence in education, career orientation and above all inculcating humbleness.

An education within the parameters of their social background, financial status and mental setup. the efforts of the Management, Staff and Parents co-operation has paid fruitful results.

Our Vision & Mission

We stands for a mother's love for her child, pure, precious, unconditional, ever giving, adorned with fine morals and values, believing in the supreme power of bonding and binding everyone with love. Describing We in a nutshell is a herculean task. The word seems to be ageless, living through ages and existing since eternity. It stands for Past, Present and Future. Before any sort of script was coined, Jaitpal was there as a divine messenger spreading love and warmth over the Universe.

Jaitpal is coming up with a huge Responsibility, a Challenge and a Mission. Responsibility towards each individual, towards their growth and towards their success. Challenge, to transform them into accomplished human beings. A Mission, to spread love, trust and bonding among them.
